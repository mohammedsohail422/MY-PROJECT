Ece Software

Though we have the necessary software available internationally for analysis, design, drafting & modeling, our MD always believed in the need to develop ‘customized’ software packs and design templates ‘in-house’ that would cater to specific project needs and clients’ ever-changing requirements. This had led to the start of an R&D department within the company in 2017. Over the years, our R&D team has developed a number of bespoke software packs and Excel-based time-saver templates. The R&D department is primarily responsible for developing, upgrading and maintaining ‘in-house’ software. Our software packs range from as complex as those for design of stiffened baseplates, anchor bolts, stand-alone footings, pedestals etc, to the ultimate time-savers such as the ones for storm water drain sizing, calculation of wind loads for PEBs and so on.

We have been providing flexible, cost-effective engineering solutions, with unparalleled speed and accuracy, to our esteemed clients. Our ‘step-by-step’ detailed calculations, in the form of design deliverables & documentation, are extremely easy to be understood by any 3rd party verifier/ PMC such as EIL, IIT, NIT and others.
